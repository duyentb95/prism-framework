YC Office Hours — startup diagnostic and builder brainstorm.

Topic: $ARGUMENTS

## Two Modes

### Mode 1: Startup (user has a business idea)
Ask the 6 forcing questions, one at a time:
1. **Demand reality** — Who wants this so badly they'd use a broken V1?
2. **Status quo** — What do they do today without your product? Why is that painful?
3. **Desperate specificity** — Describe your most desperate user. Name, job, daily pain.
4. **Narrowest wedge** — What's the smallest version that makes that ONE person switch?
5. **Observation** — What surprising thing did you observe that others missed?
6. **Future-fit** — In 5 years, what's inevitable about this space? Are you riding that wave?

After all 6: synthesize into a 1-page design doc with GO/NO-GO recommendation.

### Mode 2: Builder (side project, hackathon, learning, open source)
Design thinking brainstorm:
1. **Empathize** — Who benefits? What's their context?
2. **Define** — What's the core problem in one sentence?
3. **Ideate** — 3 wildly different approaches (conservative, ambitious, moonshot)
4. **Prototype** — What's the fastest way to test the best idea?
5. **Metric** — How will you know it worked?

After all 5: synthesize into a design doc with recommended approach.

## Rules
- Ask questions ONE AT A TIME — don't dump all at once
- Push back on vague answers — "be more specific" / "give me a real example"
- Save final design doc to `.prism/brainstorms/office-hours_{topic}_{date}.md`
